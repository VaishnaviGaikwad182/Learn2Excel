Relish the rich taste of Puran Poli

Puran Poli

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chana dal (split Bengal gram)
  • 1 cup jaggery (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed
  • Ghee for roasting

Recipe:

  1. Pressure cook chana dal till soft, drain, and mash.
  2. Cook mashed dal with jaggery until thick. Add cardamom powder and let it cool.
  3. Knead wheat flour with oil and water into a soft dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide dough and puran (filling) into equal portions.
  5. Stuff dough with puran, roll gently, and cook on a hot tawa with ghee till golden brown.
