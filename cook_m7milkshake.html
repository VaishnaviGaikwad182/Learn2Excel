Milkshakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chilled milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped fruits (banana, mango, strawberry)
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Recipe:

  1. Add chopped fruits, milk, and honey/sugar to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again for a chilled texture.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
