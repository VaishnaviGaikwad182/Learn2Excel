Enjoy healthy and tasty Upma receipe

Upma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves.
  2. Add chopped onions and green chili, sauté till soft.
  3. Add semolina and roast till fragrant.
  4. Pour in water, add salt, and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  5. Cover and cook for 3-4 minutes till upma is soft and fluffy.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney.
