Pani Puri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 20 puris (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/2 cup green chutney
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions

For Green Chutney:

  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1-inch ginger
  • 1 tsp black salt
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste

For Tamarind Chutney:

  • 1/2 cup tamarind pulp
  • 1/2 cup jaggery
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

For Puri:

  • 1 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Recipe:

  1. To prepare Green Chutney: Blend mint, coriander, green chilies, ginger, and tamarind into a smooth paste. Mix with water, cumin powder, black salt, and regular salt. Chill.
  2. To prepare Tamarind Chutney: Cook tamarind pulp with jaggery, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt until thickened.
  3. To prepare Puri: Knead a stiff dough with semolina, flour, salt, and water. Roll out small circles and deep fry until crispy.
  4. To prepare stuffing: Mix mashed potatoes, chickpeas, cumin powder, chaat masala, and salt.
  5. Poke a hole in each puri, stuff with potato mixture, add both chutneys, and dunk into the spicy water. Enjoy immediately!
