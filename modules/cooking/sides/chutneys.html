Enjoy Various types of Chutneys

Peanut Chutney

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup peanuts (roasted)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp cumin seeds (jeera seeds)

Recipe:

  1. Roast peanuts till golden, then cool.
  2. Coursely grind peanuts with garlic, red chili powder, cumin seeds and salt.

Green Chili Chutney (Thecha)

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 green chilies
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Roast green chilies and garlic in oil till blistered and fragrant.
  2. Pound or blend with salt and coriander (make it coarse for rustic texture).
  3. Mix in roasted peanuts for added texture and a slightly milder heat.

Raw Mango Chutney (Kairi Chutney)

Ingredients:

  • 1 raw mango (peeled, chopped)
  • 1 big onion chopped
  • 2 tbsp raw peanuts
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Mash mango, onion, chilies, garlic and salt together to a roughly grinded chutney.
  2. Adjust spice and salt to taste.
