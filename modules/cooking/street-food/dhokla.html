Enjoy soft and fluffy Dhokla anytime

Dhokla

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp fruit salt (eno)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe:

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, yogurt, water, turmeric, lemon juice, and salt into a smooth batter.
  2. Add fruit salt and mix gently. Pour into a greased steaming tray.
  3. Steam for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, sesame seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Pour over dhokla.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with chutney.
