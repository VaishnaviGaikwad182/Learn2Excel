Spicy Mango Pickle

Ingredients:

  • 4 raw mangoes (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds (coarsely ground)
  • 1 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tbsp nigella seeds (kalonji)
  • 1/2 tbsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil

Recipe:

  1. Marinate Mangoes: Combine chopped mangoes with turmeric and salt. Allow to sit for 1-2 days to become soft and loosen water. Drain the water.
  2. Prepare Spice Mix: Dry roast mustard, fennel, fenugreek, and nigella seeds lightly, then grind coarsely.
  3. Mix It Up: Combine the spice mix, chili powder, and hing with the mangoes.
  4. Heat Oil: Heat mustard oil until it smokes, then let cool a bit. Pour over the mango mixture and mix well.
  5. Store: Store in a glass container in sunlight for 5-7 days, shaking every few days.

Tangy Lemon Pickle

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 lemons (quartered)
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 tbsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar (optional, for balance)
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil

Recipe:

  1. Salt Cure: Mix lemon pieces with salt and turmeric.
  2. Spice Mix: Add chili powder, cumin, black pepper, and sugar.
  3. Heat Oil: Heat mustard oil till smoky, cool, and pour over the mixture.
  4. Mature: Store for 7-10 days until flavors develop.
  5. Store: Let sit in a jar for 10 days, shaking daily until the lemons soften.
