Millets

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed millets (foxtail, barnyard, or pearl millet)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil

Recipe:

  1. Rinse the millets thoroughly and drain.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add the rinsed millets and sauté for a minute.
  4. Pour in water, add salt, and bring to a boil.
  5. Cover and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the millets are cooked and water is absorbed.
  6. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.

Chana Usal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup soaked black chana (chickpeas)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and green chilies, sauté till onions turn golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  5. Add soaked black chana and enough water. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Finish with garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with rice or roti.
