Boondi Raita (Crispy Delight)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup boondi (fried chickpea balls — store-bought)
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Soak boondi in water for 5 mins (to soften), then gently squeeze out excess water.
  2. Whisk yogurt until creamy and smooth.
  3. Add soaked boondi, cumin powder, chili powder, and salt.
  4. Mix well and garnish with coriander leaves.

Onion-Tomato-Cucumber Raita

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cucumber (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

Recipe:

  1. Whisk yogurt until creamy and smooth.
  2. Add chopped onion, cucumber, tomato and regular salt.
  3. Mix well and garnish with coriander leaves.
