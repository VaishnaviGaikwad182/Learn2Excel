Treat yourself to crunchy Besan Ladoo

Besan Ladoo

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add besan, and roast on low flame till golden and aromatic.
  2. Let it cool slightly, then mix in powdered sugar, cardamom powder, and nuts.
  3. Shape into small, round ladoos while warm.
