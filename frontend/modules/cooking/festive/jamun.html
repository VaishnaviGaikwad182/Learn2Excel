Indulge in sweet and soft Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup khoya (mawa)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water

Recipe:

  1. Mix khoya, flour, baking soda, and milk to form a soft dough. Make small balls without cracks.
  2. Heat oil/ghee on low flame and fry the balls until golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling water and sugar.
  4. Soak fried gulab jamuns in warm syrup for 2 hours before serving.
