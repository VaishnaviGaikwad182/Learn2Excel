Savor spicy and buttery Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • 3 potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup peas (boiled)
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon wedges and chopped coriander (for garnish)

Recipe:

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Sauté onions, green chili, and ginger-garlic paste till fragrant.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook till soft. Add turmeric, chili powder, pav bhaji masala, and salt.
  3. Add mashed potatoes, cauliflower, and peas. Mix and mash everything together. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toast pav with butter on a pan till golden.
  5. Serve bhaji with pav, lemon wedges, and coriander on top.
