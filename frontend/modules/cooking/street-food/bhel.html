Enjoy crispy and tangy Bhel anytime

Bhel

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 1/2 cup sev
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp green chutney
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine puffed rice, onions and tomatoes.
  2. Add green chutney, tamarind chutney and chaat masala. Mix well.
  3. Top with sev and fresh coriander. Serve immediately.
