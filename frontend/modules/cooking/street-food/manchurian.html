Savor crispy Flower Manchurian with bold flavors

Flower Manchurian

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup corn flour
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup spring onions (chopped)

Recipe:

  1. Mix flour, corn flour, black pepper, and salt with water to make a thick batter.
  2. Dip cauliflower florets in batter and deep fry until golden brown.
  3. In a pan, heat oil, add garlic, ginger, and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, and vinegar. Mix well.
  5. Add fried cauliflower and toss until coated. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
