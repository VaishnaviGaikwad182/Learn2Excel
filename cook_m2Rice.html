Enjoy Staple Indian Rice Dishes

Simple White Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of any available rice
  • 1 tsp ghee (optional)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Rinse the rice well 2-3 times until the water is clear to drain excess starch.
  2. Boil water in a pot or pressure cooker. Add salt to taste.
  3. Add the rice, stir gently, and cover.
    • Pot: Cook on low heat for 10-12 minutes.
    • Pressure cooker: Cook for 3 whistles.
  4. Let the rice rest for a few minutes before fluffing with a fork.
  5. Serve hot, drizzling a little ghee and coriander leaves on top for added richness.

Jeera Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Basmati rice
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds (Jeera seeds)
  • 1 bay leaf (Tamalapatra)
  • 2 cloves (lavanga)
  • 1 small cinnamon stick (daalchini)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves (kothimbir)

Recipe:

  1. Heat ghee in the cooker on medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, and cinnamon. Let crackle.
  3. Add rice, salt, and water then stir gently.
  4. Close the lid and cook for 1 whistle on a high flame and 2 whistles on low-medium flame.
  5. Allow natural pressure release, fluff the rice, and garnish with coriander leaves.

Khichdi Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rice (regular or basmati)
  • 1/2 cup yellow moong dal (or toor dal)
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (Mohari)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (Jeera seeds)
  • 1 tsp curry leaves (kadhipatta)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp green chilies chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups water (for a soft, mushy texture)

Recipe:

  1. Wash rice and dal, soak for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in the cooker on medium heat.
  3. Add cumin, mustard seeds and allow them to crackle.
  4. Add curry leaves, sauté onions until translucent, then add green chilies, tomatoes and coriander leaves chopped.
  5. Add rice, dal and water, mix everything together.
  6. Close the lid and cook for 3 whistles on medium flame.
  7. Allow natural pressure release, fluff the rice, and garnish with coriander leaves.

Veg Masala Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Basmati rice
  • 2 tsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (Mohari)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (Jeera seeds)
  • 1 bay leaf (Tamalapatra)
  • 1 small cinnamon stick (daalchini)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup flower florets
  • 1/2 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1/2 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala (or biryani masala)
  • 1 tsp ginger, garlic and coconut paste
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves (kothimbir)

Recipe:

  1. Heat ghee in the cooker on medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, bay leaf and cinnamon. Let crackle.
  3. Sauté onions until they turn brown, add tomatoes and green chili. Cook until the tomatoes become soft.
  4. Add chopped vegetables — toss to coat with the masala.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Close the lid and cook for 1 whistle on a high flame and 2 whistles on low-medium flame.
  7. Allow natural pressure release, fluff the rice, and garnish with coriander leaves.

Tip: Mix in capsicum, corn, or paneer for a change!
